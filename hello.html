Camel



Interesting facts about camels!

  • ~ Camel's humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!
  • ~ They have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • ~ When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.

Chameleon

Interesting facts about chameleons!

  • ~ There are around 160 species of chameleon.
  • ~ Chameleons are not deaf but they do not actually have ear openings.
  • ~ Special color pigment cells under the skin called chromatophores allow some chameleon species to change their skin color!

Cicada

Interesting facts about cicadas!

  • ~ Cicada in Latin means tree cricket.
  • ~ Some cicada's are annuals meaning they come out every year or every couple of years.
  • ~ Cicadas live most of their life underground.

Panda

Interesting facts about pandas!

  • ~ Pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo.
  • ~ Pandas need at least 2 different bamboo species in their range to avoid starvation.
  • ~ At 5 months old, giant pandas learn how to climb

Pigeon

Interesting facts about pigeons!

  • ~ The fastest recorded speed of a pigeon is 92.5 mph.
  • ~ Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet.
  • ~ Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.

Teddy Bear

Interesting facts about teddy bears!

  • ~ The teddy was named after President Theodore Roosevelt, after he refused to shoot a bear during a 1902 hunting trip.
  • ~ The first teddy was made on November 14, 1902
  • ~ A teddy has been into space before!
